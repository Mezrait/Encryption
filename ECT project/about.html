About the United Arab Emirates

History

The United Arab Emirates (UAE) was formed in 1971 when seven emirates united to create a single nation. Since then, the UAE has grown into a global center for trade, tourism, and innovation.

National Symbols

National Flag

The UAE flag features four colors: red, green, white, and black, representing unity, peace, and strength.

Emblem

The UAE's emblem includes a golden falcon, symbolizing strength and heritage.

Traditional Dress

The Kandura (for men) and Abaya (for women) are traditional Emirati clothing, showcasing the rich culture and heritage of the nation.
