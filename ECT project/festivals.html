Festivals & Events in the UAE

  • National Day: Celebrated on December 2, marking the unification of the UAE.
  • Dubai Shopping Festival: A month-long shopping extravaganza attracting visitors from around the globe.
  • Abu Dhabi Film Festival: An international film festival promoting cinematic art.
  • Sharjah Light Festival: A spectacular display of lights projected onto historical buildings across Sharjah.
  • Al Dhafra Festival: A celebration of Emirati heritage, including camel races, poetry, and crafts.
